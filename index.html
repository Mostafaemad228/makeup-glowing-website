The idea of the app?

The application's idea is that it contains all the things you need in Different Egypt, whether events or transport companies or tourist and entertainment and sports places or cafés or restaurants or whatever you want you will find in the application as well as the application contains many other services aimed at facilitating the user experience and providing different features for it

What's special about other tourist apps in the app?

The app specializes in tourism in Egypt only and does not show just some events or places but is deep in them all and brings you all those places without exception together and brings you all the real information about it to make it easier for the user to know which is better and provide him with ease of choice and tourism in Egypt

Can anyone add an event or a place??

Anyone who can do that will send this information to the Admin and review it and after it is confirmed it will approve it and display it in the application

Future features of the application?

We intend to add in the future to the application several other features such as talking with admin and developing chat between users by adding other options and adding a page to show places with virtual reality technology where the tourist places are displayed in front of you by camera as well as another page to display Egyptian places and make a virtual tour inside them from within the application and other things that make the user experience inside the application better and enjoyable

Is the app official??

The app is informal and belongs to a group of Egyptian developers who aim to help their country increase its tourism status in the country and help it achieve its narrative in 2030
